About the Tests

Via our tests, you can gain insight on various aspects of your cognition and personality, and also see how much better (or not!) you perform compared to others who have taken the same tests.

Personality

Provided you answer the questions with complete honesty, you can see to what extent you are Open, Conscientious, Extraverted, Agreeable, and Neurotic.

Reaction Time / Conflict Resolution

Based on how quickly you respond to some changing stimuli presented before you, you will be tested on how different your reaction times are when there is conflict, and when there is none.

Working Memory

Working memory is a cognitive system with a limited capacity that can hold information temporarily. It is important for reasoning and the guidance of decision-making and behavior.

Non-Verbal Reasoning

Non-verbal reasoning involves the ability to understand and analyse visual information and solve problems using visual reasoning. You will be required to predict the next pattern in a series of patterns.
